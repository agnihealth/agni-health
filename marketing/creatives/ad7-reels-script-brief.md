# Ad 7 — Reels/Short-Form Video Script Brief
**Created:** 2026-05-11 | **Format:** Instagram Reels / Facebook Video  
**Angle:** Diagnostic gap — "Your doctor said you're fine. You're not."

---

## Why This Angle

Ads 1–6 have covered:
- SA-specific genetic risk (Ad 1)
- Telehealth convenience (Ad 2)
- Weight / GLP-1 (Ad 3)
- Blood sugar urgency (Ad 4)
- Before/after transformation (Ad 5/5B)

What's untapped: **the moment of awakening** — SA men who've been told their labs are "normal" but feel something is wrong, or who've seen the research and know their risk profile doesn't match their doctor's reassurance. This is high-resonance for the prediabetic-adjacent audience.

## 7-Second Version (Recommended for Testing)

### Scene Breakdown

**[0:00–0:02] — HOOK**
> **On screen (large, centered):**  
> *"Your doctor said your bloodwork is fine."*

- Black background or dark gradient
- White bold sans-serif (match Agni brand)
- No voiceover — text only, fast fade in
- Sound: none (or very subtle low hum)

---

**[0:02–0:04] — TENSION**
> **On screen (same style, text swaps):**  
> *"They didn't check HOMA-IR."*

- Brief pause for read
- Optional: red or amber accent on "HOMA-IR"
- Plays on insider knowledge — rewards SA men who know what this is

---

**[0:04–0:06] — RESOLUTION**
> **On screen:**  
> *"Agni Health does."*
> **Subtext (smaller):** *Physician-led metabolic care built for South Asian men.*

- Agni Health logo fades in
- Warm teal/amber palette shift

---

**[0:06–0:08] — CTA**
> **On screen:**  
> *"Learn more →"*  
> *agnihealth.co*

- Soft brand sound sting (optional)
- CTA button overlay from Meta

## Production Path (No Video Crew Needed)

**Option A — Motion Graphics (Recommended)**
- Use CapCut, Canva Pro, or Adobe Express
- Text-on-dark template, bold transitions
- No talent, no shoot — purely typographic
- Total production time: ~30 minutes

**Option B — Talking Head UGC Style**
- Sohan or a hired UGC creator (SA male, 30s–40s, casual)
- Script: "I went to the doctor. He said I was fine. Then I found out my HOMA-IR was 3.8. That's pre-diabetic. He never even checked it. Agni Health did."
- iPhone, ring light, neutral background
- More authentic, higher engagement ceiling

**Option C — Hybrid**
- B-roll of phone screen showing lab numbers
- Text overlays matching 7-second script
- VO optional

## Brand Compliance Checklist

- [ ] No before/after body imagery
- [ ] No stethoscopes or clinical/hospital settings  
- [ ] No claim of diagnosis or treatment guarantee
- [ ] HOMA-IR reference is educational, not diagnostic claim
- [ ] Disclaimer if using real patient lab numbers: *"Representative results. Individual outcomes vary."*
- [ ] "CA, LA & MA residents only" if targeting is national

## Audience Notes

**Primary:** SA men 30–55, interests: diabetes awareness, South Asian health, metabolic health, lab testing
**Secondary:** Retarget website visitors (pixel already live) — this ad lands hardest on warm audiences who already know the brand
