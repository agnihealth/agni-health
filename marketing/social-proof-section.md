## Design Rationale

At launch, Agni Health has zero patient testimonials (correct per policy — patient #1 must be a stranger). The social proof problem is solved with **three legitimate trust signals**:

1. **Physician credentials** — Real CV, real institutions, real training
2. **Research foundation** — MASALA 2026 is peer-reviewed, cites our exact data points
3. **Personal narrative** — Dr. Japa's own metabolic story (the body fat / protocol quote)

No fabricated testimonials. No fake review stars. All claims source-verified.

---

## Section Placement

Between "How It Works" (#3) and "About Dr. Japa" (#5) — optimal position in the trust-building arc:

> Hero → Problem (stats) → Features → How It Works → **[SOCIAL PROOF]** → About → Pricing → FAQ → CTA

This placement catches users who are thinking "this sounds right, but can I trust it?" just before the deep physician bio.
